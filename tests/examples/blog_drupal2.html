Humanitarian aid in the Middle East

Message from the Mayor

In recent days, the Mayor has shared his concerns about the heartbreaking events unfolding in Israel and Gaza.

His thoughts are with everyone affected and he continues to call on the UK Government to urgently work with international partners to secure humanitarian access and support to and from Gaza.

What you can do

The Mayor knows that many Londoners have been deeply affected by the ongoing conflict, and many have asked him how they can help.  

Here are a few organisations appealing for support: 

  • Magen David Adom UK
  • Medical Aid For Palestinians 
  • Red Cross 
  • Save The Children 
  • Unicef 
  • World Food Programme
